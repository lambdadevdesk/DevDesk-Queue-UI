Welcome to DevDesk Queue

The app designed to get you the help you need, when you need it. If you have question, issues, or inquiries related to programing or Lambda School post it here and we will assist.

Keep Organized

DevDesk Queue helps you stay organized by keeping tabs on all of your issues. You can create tickets and an administrator will be able to help you out as soon as they can get to it.

Get Help

With DevDesk Queue, you will be able to submit tickets and get the help you need, when you need it. No more having to go out to multiple locations and message multiple people for help. With the ticket system, you will be able to get help in the fastest time.
